About

I am currently doing my PhD in the Visual Computing & Artificial Intelligence Lab at the Technical University of Munich under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Matthias Niessner. I finished my bachelor's as Mechatronig engineer, then did my master's in Robotics, Cognition, Intelligence. Previously, I worked on Reinforcement Learning for control and planning. Later I dived into Active Learning for image classification. During my PhD, I am focusing on Photorealistic 3D Reconstruction, especially on lighting and material decomposition.
